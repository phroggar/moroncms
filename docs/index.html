Why Moron.CMS?

You are doing something with internet and you are asked by, let's say a band, to build a website for them. If you use a complex CMS like Wordpress or TYPO3, they surely will call you on saturday evenings and ask you, how to update their list of livegigs. Again and again.

They will never do anything else than updating this one page and they will never remember how to do it, since common Content Management Systems are way too complicated for them artsy-fartsy spirits. Better grab a onepage-theme from startbootstrap.com, put some images on the website, include Moron.CMS in the livegigs-section and you're done. Easy peasy!

Enjoy your saturday evenings! The band will be able to update their website with their smartphones while sitting in the pub down the corner. Even after 10, well, even after 20 drinks anybody including your musician friends will be able to use Moron.CMS.

It's not only made for bands of course. There are thousands of other good examples, where Moron.CMS will be the best choice to make a part of a website editable.

Like a very, very easy CMS, but easier

Hands down the easiest CMS in the galaxy! Lightweight (49 KB) and fun to use.

Easy to install

Just put the files on your server, set the file permissions and protect the edit directory. No database is required.

Easy to use

Type text or HTML in the text box and click the "update my fucking website" button. That's all. No frills - no trouble.

Mobile friendly

You can use Moron.CMS with the browser of your smartphone. Go figure!
